What to Wear & What to Know

Dress Code
Floral Formal
Garden Attire

We invite you to celebrate in florals, soft pastels, and garden hues. Gentlemen: tailored suits or blazers. Ladies: floral dresses or elegant separates. Avoid white and ivory — those belong to the bride.

A Gentle Note
Garden Terrain & Footwear

The ceremony and reception take place on lush grass and stone terraces. We kindly suggest block heels, wedges, or elegant flats over stilettos. Comfort meets elegance — you'll be dancing all night.

Transport & Parking
Complimentary Shuttle Service

Shuttles depart from Piazza della Repubblica, Florence at 10:00 AM and return at 11:30 PM. Free parking is available on-site for those travelling by car. Please RSVP your transport preference below.
